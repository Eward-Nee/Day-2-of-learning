This is an elephant:



        In lands where acacia trees abound,
        Elephants, with wisdom, roam the ground.
        With wrinkled skin and eyes so kind,
        A gentle giant, in nature we find.
        
        Trumpeting calls, a family's greet,
        In dusty herds, they gather and meet.
        Marching trunks, a sight to behold,
        Elephants, a story to be told.
        
        Ivory tusks, a symbol of might,
        Yet, a target for human plight.
        Let's protect these majestic creatures,
        For elephants, our love features.
